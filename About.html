About us


Our handcrafted offering centres around the table, a place to gather, break bread and share in the harvest  of  ideas

We believe creative expression is most powerful when forces interact: indigenous materials and techniques with a designer's inventive bursts, customary rituals with evolutions in lifestyle.Beginning with spaces that feel personal to us, our vision sees AK- potteries realized as a global community and movement towards contemporary artisanal living.



VALUES


Humane and conscious, AK-potteries is immersed in a language that revisits India's diversity by looking within.

Knowledge, sustainability and spirituality preserved over centuries are renewed as motifs, symbols and sensibilities that can travel today and touch people around the world. United by a desire to be alive to our times, paradoxical pairings establish emotional depth and dualities become whole.



AESTHETICS


Our wildly imaginative spirit is rooted in respect for India's legacy of craftsmanship and what triggers the human mind.

Through the process of co-creation, each handcrafted piece emerges from a wish for our lives to revolve around all that feeds, exhilarates and lets us dream. From ceramics and tableware to furniture, lighting and floor coverings, we champion distinctive everyday and decorative objects that make the experience of living an art. Eventually everything connects in an aesthetic that evolves from the relationships we nourish: with artisans, designers and collaborators in the present and our culture inherited from narratives of the past.
